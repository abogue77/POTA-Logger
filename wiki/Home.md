# POTA Hunter — Wiki

**POTA Hunter** is a free, open-source ham radio logging application purpose-built for [Parks on the Air (POTA)](https://parksontheair.com) activators and hunters. It runs on Windows, Linux, and macOS using only Python's standard library — no external packages required.

---

## What POTA Hunter Does

- Displays **live POTA spots** from the [POTA API](https://api.pota.app) in a real-time table
- Lets you click a spot to **auto-tune your radio** via Flrig and pre-fill the log form
- Logs QSOs to a standard **ADIF file** that can be uploaded to POTA, LOTW, or any logging software
- Shows active spots and your logged QSOs on an **interactive world map**
- Supports **QRZ.com callsign lookups** to fill in operator details
- Works as both a **hunter** (chasing activators) and an **activator** (re-spotting your own activation)
